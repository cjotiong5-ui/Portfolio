About Me

Hi! I'm Cristan Jay Otiong, a Computer Engineering student who's really into tech—lalo na yung combination ng hardware at software. I enjoy figuring out how things work behind the scenes and turning ideas into actual working systems. For me, solving problems isn't just required, it's something I actually enjoy doing.

Currently, I'm studying at Philippine College of Science and Technology, and I'm focused on improving my skills in programming, system development, and electronics. What drives me is the idea of building something useful—whether it's a system, a website, or even small projects that can make things easier or more efficient.

Outside academics, I play volleyball, grind online games like CODM and Valorant, and sometimes get into creative stuff like dancing or building side projects. I also like exploring new tech and tools, especially anything related to development and innovation, just to keep leveling up my skills.
